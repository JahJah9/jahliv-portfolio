Maybe It's Me

After a string of relationship mistakes and failed romantic endeavors, Jahliv turns the mirror on himself. Maybe It's Me follows the emotional arc of someone who starts to wonder if they're the common denominator — the problem. The short film captures that all-too-familiar cycle: the rush of excitement and hope, only to be let down once again. Raw, honest, and deeply personal — written and directed by Jahliv.
